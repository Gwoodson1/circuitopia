How to Play

Playing the Country Comparator is as easy as can be! When starting the game you will be shown two countries and a statistic for the first country. You're job is to determine if the second countries stat is higher or lower than the first country. If you guess right: congrats! You're score will go up and you'll get to keep playing. Guess wrong and thats's GAME OVER.
